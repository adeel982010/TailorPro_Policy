Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

TailorPro is a professional tailor shop management application. We collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device ID for license validation and app functionality
  • Shop Data: Shop name, address, phone number, logo
  • Customer Data: Names, phone numbers, WhatsApp, addresses, photos, custom fields
  • Order Data: Order details, measurements, pricing, status, delivery dates
  • Measurement Data: Body measurements and style preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the TailorPro application
  • License validation and trial period management
  • Data backup and synchronization (Google Drive)
  • Push notifications for order reminders

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We also store:

  • License information in secure device storage
  • Trial registration data in our Supabase cloud (for security)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • Google Drive backup (with your explicit consent)
  • Supabase for license validation

5. Google Drive Backup

When you enable backup to Google Drive, your data is stored in your own Google Drive account. You can disable this feature at any time from settings.

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To take customer and fabric photos
  • Storage: To save photos and access backups
  • Internet: For license validation and cloud backup
  • Notifications: To remind you about order deadlines

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including device-specific encryption for license information.

8. Your Rights

You can export or delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the export feature in settings
  • Uninstalling the application (data is deleted)

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@tailorpro.app

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
